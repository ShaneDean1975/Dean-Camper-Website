Florida Car Accident Laws

Florida's no-fault PIP system covers your initial medical costs, but serious injuries allow you to pursue the at-fault driver directly. Florida's modified comparative negligence law (2023) limits recovery if you are found more than 50% at fault. The statute of limitations for most car accident claims in Florida is two years from the crash date.
