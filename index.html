Our Practice Areas

At Dean & Camper Injury Lawyers, our local attorneys assist you with personal injury and wrongful death claims throughout Florida and Alabama.

Car Accidents

We handle all types of motor vehicle collisions, from fender benders to catastrophic crashes. Our team investigates every detail to build the strongest possible case for maximum compensation.

Learn More →

Truck Accidents

Commercial truck accidents cause severe injuries. We take on major trucking companies and their insurers, holding them fully accountable for negligent driving and federal safety violations.

Learn More →

Motorcycle Accidents

Motorcycle riders face unique dangers and unfair bias after accidents. We fight aggressively to overcome that bias and secure full compensation for your injuries and losses.

Learn More →

Auto Insurance Claims

When insurers deny valid claims, offer lowball settlements, or act in bad faith, we fight back. We handle UM/UIM claims, PIP disputes, and all bad-faith insurance practices.

Learn More →

Boat & Watercraft Accidents

The Gulf Coast's waterways make boating accidents a serious concern. We represent victims of boat collisions, jet ski accidents, and negligent watercraft operation throughout Florida and Alabama.

Learn More →

Wrongful Death

We help families seek justice and compensation for funeral expenses, lost income, and the immeasurable loss of companionship when a loved one is lost due to someone else's negligence.

Learn More →

Premises Liability

Property owners have a duty to maintain safe premises. We handle slip and fall accidents, inadequate security claims, dangerous conditions, and other injuries caused by negligent property owners.

Learn More →

Defective Product Injuries

Manufacturers, distributors, and retailers can be held liable for injuries caused by dangerous or defective products. We pursue full compensation against companies that put profits over safety.

Learn More →

Dog Bite Injuries

Florida's strict liability law holds dog owners responsible for bites and attacks regardless of prior behavior. We fight to recover medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering damages.

Learn More →

Other Personal Injuries

We handle a wide range of injury cases including pedestrian accidents, bicycle crashes, nursing home abuse, traumatic brain injuries, spinal cord injuries, and catastrophic injury claims.

Learn More →
